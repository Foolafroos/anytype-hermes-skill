# Privacy Policy — Anytype Hermes Skill

**Last updated:** May 2026

## Overview
This is a Hermes Agent skill for integrating with Anytype via MCP. All communication is between your local Hermes instance and your local Anytype server.

## Data Handling
- MCP communication stays on localhost (port 31009)
- Anytype data (notes, spaces, pages) never leaves your machine
- No telemetry or analytics

## Your Data
- Bearer tokens are stored in your local `.env` file
- All Anytype content remains in your Anytype vault

## Third-Party Services
- None. Local-to-local communication only.
